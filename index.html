About Me

Hi, I'm Tyrone from Hampton, Virginia. I'm currently studying computer science at Norfolk State University, and I have a deep interest in Software Development, UI/UX Design, Information Technology, Cybersecurity and Project Management.

I am eager to contribute my expertise, gain hands-on experience, and further my development in this field.

As a dedicated scholar, I have excelled in my coursework and actively participated in research projects. My commitment to academic excellence has been recognized through various scholarships and awards, reflecting my dedication and hard work in the field of computer science.

Besides my academic achievements, I have extensive experience designing and developing mobile applications and websites. My projects include creating user-friendly interfaces and ensuring seamless user experiences across different devices. I use modern technologies and frameworks such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Bootstrap to build responsive and visually appealing websites.

One of my notable projects was a bus shuttle application developed as part of a software engineering class. This project allowed me to apply my skills in organizing, facilitating meetings, and presenting implementation ideas, contributing to the project's success.

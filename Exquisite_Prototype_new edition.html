Our Services

We offer Intensive In-Home Services aimed at enhancing family functioning and addressing significant impairments in major life activities. Common issues addressed include, but are not limited to:

  • Anger Management
  • Anxiety and Depression
  • Alcohol and Drug Abuse
  • Child Discipline Techniques
  • Interpersonal Conflicts
  • Delinquent Behaviors
  • Grief and Loss
  • School Attendance
  • Parent/Child Conflicts

Intensive In-Home Services

  • These services aim to enhance family functioning and address significant impairments in major life activities resulting from the individual’s mental, behavioral, and/or emotional challenges.
  • Services are usually time-limited, provided typically in the residence of an individual who is at risk of being moved to out-of-home placement or who is being transitioned back home from out-of-home placement.
  • Individuals must also demonstrate significant impairments of functioning in which impacts their major life activities.
  • These services include crisis treatment; individual and family counseling; life, parenting, and communication skills; case management activities and coordination with other services; and 24/7 emergency response.
